Booking Policy

The booking will be deposited at 5% and 95% will be paid directly to the hotel on arrival . In the event of a cancellation, this deposit will be refunded when the reservation is cancelled through website and based on Booking Cancellation Policy.

cancellation Policy

Any cancellation received within 3 days prior to arrive will be incur the first night . Failure to arrive at your hotel will be treated as a No-show and no refund will be given.
